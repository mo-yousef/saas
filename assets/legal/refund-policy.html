Refund Policy for NordBK

Last Updated: September 7, 2025

1. Overview

We offer a 14-day free trial for our subscription plans. You can cancel your subscription at any time. If you cancel your subscription, you will continue to have access to the service until the end of your billing period. We do not offer refunds for subscription fees already paid.

2. Refunds for Non-Subscription Purchases

For any non-subscription purchases, our policy lasts 30 days. If 30 days have gone by since your purchase, unfortunately, we can’t offer you a refund or exchange.

3. Late or missing refunds (if applicable)

If you haven’t received a refund yet, first check your bank account again. Then contact your credit card company, it may take some time before your refund is officially posted. Next, contact your bank. There is often some processing time before a refund is posted. If you’ve done all of this and you still have not received your refund yet, please contact us at support@nordbk.com.

4. Contact Us

If you have any questions about our Refund Policy, please contact us at: support@nordbk.com
